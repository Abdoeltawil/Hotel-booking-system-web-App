Hilton Hurghada Resort

Featuring exclusive access to the Red Sea, Hotel Hilton Hurghada Resortname boasts three pools, a watersports centre, and a dive school where guests can learn how to scuba dive. All rooms at this hotel enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi, and flat-screen TVs with satellite channels are standard. Work desks are provided in all accommodation, as are tea/coffee making facilities. Guests looking to exercise can make use of the modern fitness centres, or swim in the lap pool. Facilities also include a paddle tennis table, playground, and squash and tennis courts.
