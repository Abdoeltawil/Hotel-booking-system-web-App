Four Seasons Hotel

Four Seasons Hotel Alexandria at San Stefanoname is a luxury, waterfront hotel in Alexandria. The property features stunning sea and city views and amenities include an outdoor infinity pool, indoor pool, and private beach area. All rooms are air-conditioned and feature marble en suite bathrooms and balconies overlooking either the city or the Mediterranean Sea. Other amenities include Wi-Fi, safes, satellite television, and tea and coffee facilities. The spa has a hot tub and sauna and offers massages, facials and other treatments. A kid's club, fitness centre, squash court and meeting facilities all feature. Lebanese and Syrian dishes are featured at Byblos Restaurant while a la carte and buffet options are available for breakfast at Kala Restaurant.
