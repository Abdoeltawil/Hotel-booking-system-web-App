Kempinski Nile Hotel

Located in Garden City district of Cairo, Kempinski Nile Hotel Caironame is a 5-star boutique hotel that offers deluxe accommodation on the banks of river Nile. With magnificent views, the suites feature LCD TV, complimentary minibar and a marble bathroom. Additionally, the hotel provides pillow concierge and butler service. Ultimate relaxation can be experienced at The Spa that specialises in seasonal body massage and personal trainer for a workout program. A lazy afternoon can be enjoyed with a dip the rooftop swimming pool.
